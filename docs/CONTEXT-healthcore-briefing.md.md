# Company Choice: HealthCore

**Why HealthCore?**

I have chosen HealthCore because I currently work in the healthcare sector, which provides me with a solid understanding of its workflow dynamics, clinical terminology, and day-to-day operational challenges. My experience helps me understand how to bridge disparate digital patient records while ensuring confidentiality regulations are strictly upheld, bringing a realistic perspective of clinical operations. I am highly motivated to apply AI engineering within an environment I know well to solve critical issues like administrative burden, transforming technology into a true ally that returns quality time to healthcare professionals and enhances patient care.

**Departments of Interest:**

1. **Revenue Cycle and Billing:** The challenge of reducing the 14% claim rejection rate through an AI-assisted review system is a use case with a direct and measurable financial impact.
2. **Patient Experience and Access:** Addressing the 22% no-show rate using predictive modeling and smart reminder systems represents an ideal opportunity to apply advanced analytics.

**AI or Automation Challenge from the Milestone Map:**

The milestone I am most excited to build is **AI-Assisted Clinical Documentation**. Reducing the 35 minutes that clinicians spend daily on administrative tasks by leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) is, in my view, the most valuable application of generative AI in this context, as it directly improves operational efficiency and staff satisfaction.

I am also highly drawn to developing an **AI-Assisted Claim Review System**. This challenge involves creating a solution capable of flagging high-risk submissions before they are sent, providing automated coding suggestions based on clinical notes, and consolidating a unified billing dashboard that displays US and UK revenue in real time. Implementing denial pattern analysis and automated follow-up workflows for unpaid claims will be fundamental to resolving the company's revenue leaks.
